How We Work
Full-Cycle Metal Solutions

We operate across the entire metal recycling and sourcing value chain, providing tailored solutions for producers, traders, and processors.

01

Collection & Logistics

We manage collection, transport, and delivery of scrap and processed metals across Greece and Europe, with full ADR and waste transport compliance.

02

Grading & Inspection

Thorough material inspection and content analysis for all traded commodities. Detailed reporting ensures quality, purity, and contractual compliance.

03

Import & Export

Active involvement in cross-border metal trading. We handle customs documentation, export licenses, and international logistics for efficient recovery and redistribution.

04

Raw Material Procurement

Leveraging a network of European and global suppliers, we source specific metals and feedstocks for manufacturers who require reliable, spec-compliant supply.

05

Environmental Compliance

We coordinate directly with competent authorities to ensure all collection, transport, and recovery activities comply with EU and national environmental law.

06

Consulting & Advisory

Expert guidance on metal waste classification, regulatory obligations, and optimising the commercial value of your waste streams and by-products.
