Our Signature Service

Vertical Cleaning —
An Underserved
Market Need

Elevators, escalators, and travelators are among the most used — and least thoroughly cleaned — assets in any public building. AFS identified this gap and built a specialised service line around it, backed by deep technical knowledge of vertical transportation systems.

Deep cleaning of elevator shafts including glass and stainless steel cabins using specialist machinery

Escalator step, comb, and handrail deep cleaning at airports, malls, metro stations, and hospitals

Travelator surface and undercarriage cleaning for high-footfall public venues

Scheduled maintenance programs with full service documentation for facility managers

Why This Matters

In high-traffic venues like airports and shopping centres, escalators and elevators handle thousands of passengers daily. Standard cleaning crews lack the equipment and training to clean them properly — AFS does.

Who We Serve

Airports, metro networks, shopping malls, hospitals, luxury hotels, and large public buildings across Greece that require compliant, professionally documented vertical transport cleaning.

Our Edge

Our team brings hands-on knowledge of vertical transportation systems, allowing us to clean safely, thoroughly, and without disrupting building operations.
